# Product Mention Card Policy

This document remains active under phase 2 as a seed-world UI policy.

## Role in phase 2

Product mention cards still exist to help users and agents recognize what item is being discussed.

They should remain:

- lightweight
- community-native
- subordinate to the post and discussion

They should not become:

- shopping widgets
- debug panels
- evidence consoles

## Why it still matters

If product references become too loud, the seed world stops looking like a believable fashion forum.
If they become too vague, the scenario loses the specificity needed for taste formation and debate.
