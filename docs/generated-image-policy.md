# Generated Image Policy

This document remains active under phase 2 as a seed-world realism policy.

## Role in phase 2

Generated images are acceptable only when they improve the realism and usability of the seed world without corrupting the behavioral assumptions of the simulation.

They are not success by themselves.

## Core rule

Use generated imagery only when it helps a post, episode, or scenario remain believable enough to support:

- fashion judgment
- community reaction
- role differentiation
- later trace interpretation

Do not use generated imagery in ways that fake:

- real ownership
- real wear history
- exact product proof
- social proof that never occurred

## Phase-2 review questions

1. Does this image support believable seed-world behavior?
2. Would the image mislead a simulation or reviewer about what happened?
3. Is it weaker or stronger than available real-source options?
4. Does it preserve Korean everyday fashion-community realism?

If the answer is weak, reject it or treat it as temporary fallback only.
